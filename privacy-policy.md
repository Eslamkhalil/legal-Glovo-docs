
# Privacy Policy for Glovo (Company Version)

**Effective Date:** March 16, 2026  
**Last Updated:** March 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how **Glovo Inc.** ("the Company", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information through our mobile application and services.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
To provide a reliable ride-sharing service, we collect:
* **Identity Data:** Full name, government-issued ID (for drivers), and contact details.
* **Geo-Location Data:** We collect precise location data (GPS) from riders and drivers. This includes **Background Location** data to enable trip tracking, safety features, and fair pricing even when the app is minimized.
* **Financial Data:** Payment methods and transaction history processed through encrypted third-party providers.

## 2. Data Sharing and Disclosure
We do not sell your personal data. Data is only shared with:
* Drivers (to facilitate the ride).
* Service providers (cloud hosting, payment gateways).
* Law enforcement (when legally required).

## 3. Data Security
The Company implements industry-standard SSL encryption and security protocols to protect user data against unauthorized access or disclosure.

## 4. User Rights
Users have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of their personal data through the app settings or by contacting our legal department.
